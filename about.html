This purpose of this website is to teach about Astrophotography and also to get people interested in it. We work to expand the website as time moves on to provide more guides and more user-submitted photography for you, without any cost! We are currently not interested in making a profit and the costs of running this website is very low. We are just doing it for fun and for your pleasure! It is in our main interest that you, the users, find this website entertaining and informational.

Contact us if you have any questions or suggestions for improvements on our website. You will find contact information under the contact tab!
